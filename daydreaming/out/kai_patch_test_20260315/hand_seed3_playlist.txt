name: Letter on the table
situation: sit_unopened_letter
option_effect: inspect
visual_description: The letter stays unopened on the table while the kitchen gives Kai smaller tasks to do instead of answering it.

cycle 2: k_patch_anchor
name: Towel folded instead of opening
situation: sit_unopened_letter
option_effect: inspect
visual_description: Kai folds the dish towel again instead of touching the letter, drinks water at the sink, and lets the kitchen keep him facing everywhere except the table. The harbor comes back to him through his hands before he admits it with language, and he reaches for the towel again as if the next straightened crease might count as a reply.

name: Mug over the handwriting
situation: sit_unopened_letter
option_effect: prepare
visual_description: He sets his mug over one corner of the envelope so the handwriting disappears, wipes the same spot twice, and remembers too clearly what silence did the last time he let it stand in for an answer. The thought does not move him yet, but it turns the kitchen from cover into a threshold.
