## High-level read

The batch works mechanically and produces real material variation, but it is still a narrow pool.

What it does well:
- stable `avoidance` geometry across all five sequences
- strong domestic-ritual texture
- consistent psychological pressure: `attachment_threat`
- clear harbor contamination across the strongest nodes
- graph-valid, contamination-clean nodes

What it does not do yet:
- meaningful progression beyond repeated delay rituals
- payoff closure or route opening
- pressure diversity
- practice diversity
- much real difference in `option_effect` (`none` dominates)

So the batch is good enough as a material-supply smoke test, but not yet a rich curation test.
