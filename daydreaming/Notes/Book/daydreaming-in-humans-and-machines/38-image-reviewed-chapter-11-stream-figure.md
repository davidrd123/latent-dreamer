## Figure 11.1: Overdetermined Stream of Consciousness

PDF page: `300`

The figure shows several horizontal process paths made of linked square nodes. These paths split and merge, and a dotted box marks one local region labeled:

- `stream of consciousness`

The visual claim is that the stream of consciousness is not a single simple line. Instead, it is a small local outcome of multiple partially independent planning and inference processes that:

- branch
- merge
- compete
- jointly determine what becomes the currently experienced thought stream

The boxed region matters. It frames the stream of consciousness as a local emergent slice cut out of a larger overdetermined process network.
